## ğŸ¨ Customization Guide

### Update Your Information

1. **Personal Details**
   - Open `portfolio.html` in your text editor
   - Update your name, title, and bio in the relevant sections

2. **Projects Section**
   - Add your project descriptions
   - Update project links and images
   - Showcase your best work

3. **Skills Section**
   - List your technical skills
   - Add or remove skills as needed

4. **Contact Information**
   - Update email address
   - Add social media links (LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter, etc.)

### Modify Styling

- Colors: Search for color codes in the CSS and replace with your preferred palette
- Fonts: Update font families in the CSS
- Layout: Adjust spacing, padding, and margins to your liking
- Sections: Add or remove sections based on your needs

## ğŸŒ Deployment

### GitHub Pages (Recommended)

1. Go to your repository on GitHub
2. Click on **Settings**
3. Scroll down to **GitHub Pages** section
4. Under **Source**, select `main` branch
5. Click **Save**
6. Your site will be live at `https://yourusername.github.io/Simple-Portfolio/`

- **Netlify**: Drag and drop your folder to [Netlify Drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
